Pila®

Personal Finance Manager

Pila is a simple finance manager designed for controlling personal expenses. The app allows storing information about the expenses, such as date, value, amount, and tags. Tags are keywords used to describe the nature of the expenses. Tags that are used once are stored e presented as a suggestion in future typings.

The app is open source under the Apache License and was initially developed by the class of the course Basic Android Developed offered by the FTEC college in October 2012. Here is a list with the names of the original authors:
